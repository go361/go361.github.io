2018年第5周:
本周只有前三天完成了翻背和复习任务；周四、周五只完成了翻背任务；周六的翻背任务拖延到周天才完成；复盘拖延至第六周周一完成。累计语音输出786秒，约18分钟。

##L73 The way to King Street
###1. and 表示结果
她对伦敦不很熟悉，所以她迷路了。
She doesn’t know London well, so she lost her way.
She doesn’t know London very well, and she lost her way.
句中，and 当“所以”讲，表示结果。
###2. bus stop 和 bus station
**bus stop:**a place at the side of a road that is marked with a sign, where buses stop. 公共汽车停靠站
**bus station:**the place in a town or city where buses(especially to or from other town) leave and arrive. 公共汽车站；(尤指)长途汽车站
###3. say to oneself 心中暗想
talk to oneself 意为“自言自语地说”
She thought, “I can ask him the way.”
“I can ask him the way.” she said to herself.
###4. friendly 和 pleasantly
friendly: adj. behaving in a kind and pleasant way because you like sb or want to help them.
pleasant:
enjoyable, pleasing or attractive 令人愉快的；可喜的；宜人的；吸引人的
friendly and polite 友好的；和善的；文雅的
The man smiled friendly.
The man smiled pleasantly.

Can you find a pair for me?

##L81 Roastbeef ang Potatoes

**nearly:**almost; not quite; not completely
have(完全动词): have 可以替代常用动词，表示 eta，enjoy，experience，drink，take等意义。这时的have是行为动词，因此，可以用于各种时态。

pack: verb : to put clothes,etc. into a bag in preparation for a trip away from home 收拾(行李)；装(箱)
When do you plan to have a holiday?
When are you going to have a holiday?
